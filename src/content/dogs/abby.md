# Abby

## Meet Abby

Abby is a 7-year-old brindle Boxer with an incredibly calm, easygoing personality. She is one of those dogs who settles into a home almost immediately and asks for very little besides a comfortable place to relax and someone to spend time with.

She is quiet, affectionate, and wonderfully low-maintenance. If you're looking for a companion who enjoys being with you without constantly demanding attention, Abby may be the perfect match.

# Personality

Abby is exceptionally chill.

She has been one of the easiest foster dogs to care for. She is content to relax, rarely vocalizes, and transitions into new environments surprisingly well considering the major life change she's recently experienced.

She is calm inside the home and has shown herself to be a very low-drama companion.

---

# Around the House

Abby has been fantastic indoors.

- Doesn't bark
- Doesn't whine
- Sleeps quietly
- Excellent in her crate
- House trained
- Easy to live with

---

# Walking

Abby enjoys walks but is a fairly strong girl and can pull on leash.

She is also sensitive to sudden loud noises and unfamiliar surfaces.

One thing we've noticed is that metal grates (such as storm drains or sidewalk grates) can be scary for her. She'll often avoid them or become startled if they make noise. With patience and encouragement she's continuing to build confidence.

---

# Other Dogs

Abby has done well with other dogs.

Rescue evaluations described her as playful and appropriately social. She reads other dogs well and responds appropriately to corrections.

---

# Her Story

Abby and her sister entered rescue after their owner became disabled and could no longer care for them.

Despite losing the only home she'd known, Abby has remained gentle, trusting, and incredibly sweet.

---

# Ideal Home

Abby would likely do well with someone looking for:

- A calm companion
- A lower-energy adult Boxer
- A dog that settles nicely in the home
- Someone willing to continue building her confidence on walks
